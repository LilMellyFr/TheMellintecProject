# MLTC - 0.1.7a
**last push: 04.01.2025 20:08**

**MLTC** is an ambitious project designed to **enhance school breaks** by giving students access to games and educational entertainment in a safe, unblocked environment.

---

## Features

- Access to **30+ unblocked sites**.  
- Play **over 10,000 high-quality games**.  
- Includes a **fully playable version of Minecraft 1.12.2**.  

> Special thanks to **Eaglercraft** for providing the files for the Minecraft clone.

---

## Why MLTC?

MLTC is built to **bring enjoyment back to school breaks**, providing a curated selection of games and experiences that are both engaging and safe.

---

## Get Started

Simply browse the available sites, pick your favorite games, and enjoy your break.
